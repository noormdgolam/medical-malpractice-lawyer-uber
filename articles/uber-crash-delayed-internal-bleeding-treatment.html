Evidentiary Requirements

To establish a successful medical malpractice claim, you'll need to provide evidence of the following:

  • Causation: Proof that the delayed treatment caused harm or injury to the patient.
  • Damages: Documentation of the patient's injuries, medical expenses, and any other related damages.

Evidentiary Requirements for Medical Malpractice

To establish a medical malpractice claim, you must demonstrate the following:

  • Duty of care: The medical provider had a duty to provide a standard level of care.
  • Breach of duty: The medical provider failed to meet the standard of care, resulting in harm to the patient.
  • Causation: The breach of duty caused the patient's injuries or conditions.
  • Damages: The patient suffered actual harm or injuries as a result of the breach of duty.
